How do you curate articles?

Articles are hand selected. Everything that calls my attention and passes the «trivia filter» will be added to Slower News. It's certainly affected by my biases (I'm aware of these) and most articles were on Hacker News.

My strategy is to pick articles that are relevant at least for some months: articles that dissect trends, unveil lesser known trends or some interesting edge cases and counter-examples. The articles listing order is also not random.

How often is Slower News updated?

Only when relevant. Visit every month and «you're good to go». New articles are easy to spot and there is RSS also.

Can I contribute?

Open an issue or pull request the repository with relevant links, corrections or suggestions. I plan to curate only two pages (this and the main page) but I accept pull requests creating/updating more - if you can curate them and will handle the burden of using HTML. For now this is my filter to accept them.
